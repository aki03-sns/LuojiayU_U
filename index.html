Starbox Starbox

Claude Code HTML/CSS/JS Vercel
Sometimes when I'm feeling down, I want to write something, but just writing without any response feels boring. I love chatting with AI. I've seen people making pen pals on social media, but I'm too shy to pour my heart out to strangers — I'm always afraid they're secretly judging me. One day it hit me: why not combine writing with AI? And so, Starbox was born. 有时候心情不好的时候会想写点什么，但只是单纯地写点什么又觉得有点无聊，希望能得到一点回应。我喜欢和AI聊天。我看见很多人都在社交软件上交笔友，但是我不好意思和别人说那么多话，而且我总害怕别人在心理评判我。有一天我突发奇想，为什么不能写点什么和AI结合在一起呢？于是它诞生了。
A website where AI writes back randomly. You pour out whatever's on your mind, and the system picks a random AI character to reply. Each character responds based on their own personality and backstory. You might get a reply that drives you crazy, or you might stumble upon a delightful surprise — like opening a blind box. 这是一个AI随机回信的网站。你写下你想说的话，系统会随机抽取一个AI角色回信。角色会根据他的人物设定和环境设定生成回信。可能会被他的回信气个半死，也可能会收获到意外之喜。像开盲盒一样。
I independently led the product definition and all design decisions. Built and deployed entirely with AI programming tools. 从产品定义到决策都是我独立主导的，通过AI编程工具完成开发和部署。
